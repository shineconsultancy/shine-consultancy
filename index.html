Monica
Semedo

I have been catching audiences since before I could explain how — and I have spent a lifetime refining that instinct into something I can now give to others.

For 22 years the face of RTL Télé Lëtzebuerg — starting at age 11, anchoring news, hosting shows, presenting live events. Master of Ceremonies at the Olympic Games of the Small States of Europe, Ironman 70.3, Philharmonie Luxembourg and major corporate events. Member of the European Parliament 2019–2024, where I chaired sessions, spoke in plenary and represented Luxembourg on the international stage. Speaker at the FRA Forum Vienna, Reykjavik Global Forum and UN pre-Summit Geneva. Featured on CNN, BBC and Euronews.

Through all of it, I learned something no course teaches: how to hold a room not by being louder — but by being fully, authentically present. That is what I now teach through SHINE Stage Confidence®.

Group Learning

Workshop
Formats.

Each workshop is adapted to your organisation’s context, sector and real communication challenges. Available as a half-day (4h) or full-day format. Ideal group size: 6–10 participants.

Workshop 01
Lead with Clarity
Half-day or Full-day · 6–10 participants

Strengthen how leaders structure and deliver their core message in professional settings. Board updates, strategy briefings, client presentations and internal announcements.

  • Core message extraction in 60 seconds
  • Structured response framework
  • 90-second executive intervention
  • Live Q&A and panel simulation
Workshop 02
Engage the Room
Half-day or Full-day · 6–10 participants

Develop confident and engaging presence in group communication settings. Conferences, town halls, panels, leadership events and large internal presentations.

  • Speech architecture that holds attention
  • The Diamond Upgrade — storytelling for professionals
  • Stage positioning and audience connection
  • Voice, pauses and live repetition rounds
Workshop 03
Confident Visibility
Half-day or Full-day · 6–10 participants

Prepare leaders for visible communication moments with clarity and composure. Media interviews, corporate videos, public statements and internal video messages.

  • Message framing for visibility
  • Structured interview responses
  • Camera practice — record, review, refine
  • Complex question simulation
